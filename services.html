What We Do

Our Services

Proper tree care is an investment that can lead to substantial returns. We provide comprehensive tree and landscape services across the Wasatch Front.

Core Expertise

Tree Care Services

Tree & Plant Healthcare

Plants can become sick due to insects and disease. We have the education, knowledge, and experience to diagnose plant problems correctly and prescribe effective treatment programs. With over 75 years of experience, Behunin Tree is the best choice for the care of your trees.

Tree Pruning & Trimming

Correct pruning practices are essential — they can mean life or death for your trees. Proper pruning improves the health and appearance of trees and prolongs their useful life by removing dead, weakened, interfering, diseased, or insect-infested branches. We follow ANSI A300 standards with over 75 years of proper pruning experience.

Tree Removal

Professional tree removal without causing unnecessary damage to surrounding vegetation or structures. When trees become hazardous, diseased beyond treatment, or need to be cleared for construction, our certified team handles the job safely, efficiently, and thoroughly.

Tree Transplanting

Expert tree relocation services to preserve mature trees when construction or landscaping changes require it. We carefully extract, transport, and replant trees to give them the best chance of thriving in their new location.

Shrub Pruning & Trimming

Proper pruning can have a significant impact on the health and life of your shrubs and plants. Our experts provide careful, knowledgeable care for all your ornamental plantings to keep them healthy and beautiful year-round.

WATS Chemical Treatment

Chemical applications that decrease water use on lawns and reduce the frequency of mowing — saving you time and resources. An innovative approach to lawn maintenance in Utah's dry climate.

Beyond Trees

Landscape & Outdoor Services

Landscape Planning & Design

Landscaping is more than just trees, shrubs, and plants. We take into consideration how everything gets hydrated, water flow, shading, and the best plants for your property. A thoughtful plan ensures your landscape thrives for years to come.

Outdoor Living Spaces

Pools, rock walls, pavers, kitchens, outdoor BBQs — creating your own outdoor room is more affordable than you might think. As a Coyote Outdoor Living, ClifRock, and Bull Grills dealer, we bring your dream outdoor space to life.

Sprinkler Systems

We design, build, fix, and maintain both commercial and residential sprinkler and hydration systems in alignment with your landscaping plan. Proper irrigation is the foundation of a healthy landscape.

Hydroseeding

A much more affordable alternative to sod or grass, utilizing a slurry of seed and mulch for effective and uniform lawn establishment. Get a lush, green lawn at a fraction of the cost.

Annual Flower Planting

Decades of expertise in flower box planting, flower pot ideas, and both cultivation and care for beautiful seasonal displays. We bring vibrant color to your property year after year.

Holiday & Event Lighting

The ones that started holiday lighting in Utah. From Temple Square and the Delta Center to the 2002 Winter Olympics and Draper's Tree of Life. Let us transform your property into a winter wonderland.
